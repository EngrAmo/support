  # EngrAmo Support

  Support and issue tracker for [EngrAmo](https://apps.apple.com/app/engramo) — a spaced-repetition
  flashcard app for iOS and Android.

  ## Reporting a bug or requesting a feature

  Please [open an issue](../../issues/new) and include:

  - What you expected to happen vs. what actually happened
  - Steps to reproduce
  - Your device model and OS version (e.g. iPhone 15, iOS 17.5)
  - App version (Settings → About)

  ## Other questions

  For anything not covered by an issue, reach out at support@engramo.app.
